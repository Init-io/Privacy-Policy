SMS Forwarder — Account & Data Deletion Instructions

Users can delete their account and all associated data directly within the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

What data is deleted

When a user deletes their account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile information
  • All user data
  • Preferences
  • Authentication credentials

Need help?

If you need assistance with account deletion, please contact the developers at developers.init.io@gmail.com.

Note: Deleting your account will permanently remove the items listed above. If you believe you need additional help, include the email address associated with your account in your message to speed up support.
