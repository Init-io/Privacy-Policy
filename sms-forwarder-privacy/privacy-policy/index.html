Privacy Policy for SMS Forwarder

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The app collects incoming SMS messages from your device. We do not access contacts, call logs, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

SMS messages are sent to a secure Firebase database so you can access them later. We use the data only for the purpose of allowing you to view your messages from other devices.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

Your SMS messages are not shared, sold, or distributed to third parties. Data is stored securely in Firebase and can only be accessed by your account.

4. Permissions

The app requires SMS read permission. SMS access is optional. The app only works when you grant permission.

5. Data Retention

Messages remain stored in your Firebase account until you delete them.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data in transit and at rest.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified in-app if any changes occur.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: developers.init.io@gmail.com
